Why the Cookbook project?

This website aims to promote healty eating and cooking with focus on easy to prepare and quick homemade meals. While creating the website and uploading the recipes in the beggining, we have tried to have in mind the fast-paced daily routines of the cities we live in. This always leads to having so little time for homemade comforting meal. Hence, this idea was brought to life. Please join to share or check out recipes and enjoy.
